Peanut Butter Banana Quesadillas

Description

These peanut butter banana quesadillas are a light yet satisfying breakfast concoction made with two longtime staples: peanut butter and banana. A perfect start to a relaxing weekend day when coupled with a glass of Moscato and a bowl of chocolate-covered fruit.

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp peanut butter
  • 2 (8 inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Spread 2 tablespoons peanut butter onto each tortilla. Top one tortilla with banana slices, then cover with remaining tortilla, peanut butter side down.
  2. Spray cooking spray into a skillet and heat over medium heat. Place quesadilla into the hot skillet and cook until lightly browned, about 3 minutes per side.
  3. Cut in half to serve.
  4. Add your own kick to your liking, such as fruit on top with whipped cream or melted milk chocolate.
