FRUIT SMOOTHIES: MADE WITH XTRA LOVE BY HARPER BEAN<3

DESCRIPTION

Indulge in delightful fruit smoothies made with love by Harper Bean, blending together the freshest and most luscious fruits to create a truly refreshing and heartwarming experience. She uses EXTRA ingredients that'll shoot passed your taste buds and right into your HEART!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of frozen Fruit Medley
  • 1 full banana
  • fresh strawberries
  • handful blueberries, raspberries, or blackberries (or any fresh fruit of your choosing)
  • 1 1/2 cup milk (any kind), half and half, or heavy cream
  • tbsp yoguart
  • 1/2 cup sugar

Steps

  1. In 6 cup blender, mix all ingredients together, leaving out some fruit is okay if you don't have a certain kind, it won't make a difference
  2. Blend using the smoothie option on high speed for 1 minute
  3. Pour into cup
  4. Top with whipped cream if you'd like, and lots of it!
